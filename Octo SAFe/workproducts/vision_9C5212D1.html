Outcome: Vision
Vision is a description of the future state of the Solution under development. It reflects Customer and stakeholder needs, as well as the Feature and Capabilities, proposed to meet those needs. The Vision applies to any level of SAFe and is both aspirational and achievable, providing the broader context - an overview and purpose - of the solution under development.
