Main Description

The epic burn-up chart tracks progress toward an epic's completion. There are three measures:

-Initial estimate: estimated Story points from the Lean business case

-Work completed: actual Story points rolled up from the epic's child Features and stories

-Cumulative work completed: Cumulative story points completed and rolled up from the epic's child features and stories

Key Considerations
Metrics have different implementations for each level: Portfolio, Program, Large Solution, and Team.
