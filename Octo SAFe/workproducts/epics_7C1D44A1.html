Artifact: Epics
An Epic is a container for a Solution development initiative large enough to require analysis, the definition of a Minimum Viable Product (MVP), and financial approval prior to implementation.
Work Product Kinds: Work Products
Purpose
Epics are the containers that capture and manage the largest initiatives that occur within a portfolio.
