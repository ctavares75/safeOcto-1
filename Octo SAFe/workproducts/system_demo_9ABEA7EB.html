Artifact: System Demo
The System Demo is a significant event that provides an integrated view of new Features for the most recent Iteration delivered by all the teams in the Agile Release Train (ART). Each demo gives ART stakeholders an objective measure of progress during a Program Increment (PI).
Work Product Kinds: Work Products
Purpose

The purpose of the system demo is to test and evaluate the full system in the staging environment that the ART is working on and getting feedback from the primary stakeholders. These stakeholders include Business Owners, executive sponsers, other Agile Teams, development management, customers who provide input on the fitness for the purpose for the solution under development. The feedback is critical, as only they can give the guidance the train needs to stay on course or take corrective action.
