Role: Development Manager
The development manager (or engineer manager for system development) is a manager who exhibits the principles and practices of Lean-Agile leadership. Further, the manager has personal responsibility for the coaching and career development of direct reports, takes responsibility for eliminating impediments, and actively evolves the systems in which all knowledge workers operate. They have final accountability for effective value delivery as well.
Role Sets: Agile PMO, Agile Portfolio Operations, Lean Governance
Relationships
Primary Performs
  • Building High-Performance Teams and Individuals
  • Inspect and Adapt Workshops
  • Maintaining Appropriate Distance from Team
  • Participating in Defining and Administering Compensation, Benefits, and Promotions
  • Participating in Post-PI Planning
  • Participating in Pre-PI Planning
  • Performing Career Counseling and Personal Development
  • Protecting Teams from Distractions
  • Serving as Agile Coach Advisor to Agile Teams
