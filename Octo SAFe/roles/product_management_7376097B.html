Role: Product Management
Product Management has content authority for the Program Backlog. They are responsible for identifying Customer needs, prioritizing Features, guiding the work through the Program Kanban and developing the program Vision and Roadmap.
Role Sets: Agile PMO, Agile Portfolio Operations, Lean Agile Center of Excellence (LACE), Lean Governance , Roles
Relationships
Primary Performs
  • Building an Effective Product Management/Product Owner Team
  • Contribution to the Solution Demo
  • Defining Releases and Program Increments
  • Developing and Communicating Program Vision and Roadmap
  • Inspect and Adapt Workshops
  • Managing and Prioritizing the Flow of Work
  • Participating in Post-PI Planning
  • Participating in Pre-PI Planning
  • Program Backlog Refinement
  • Program Increment (PI) Planning
  • Scope, Time, and Constraint Managment
  • Understanding and Supporting Portfolio Work
  • Understanding Customer Needs and Validating Solutions
  • Working With System Architect/Engineering to Understand Enabler Work
Main Description
Product Management involves the internal voice of the Customer and works with Customers and Product Owners to understand and communicate their needs, define system features, and participate in validation. They are responsible for the program backlog.
Staffing
SkillsPersonability with customers, vision-oriented, flixible
Key Considerations
While the roles of Product Management and Solution Management are similar in most aspects, they manage different levels of concern.
