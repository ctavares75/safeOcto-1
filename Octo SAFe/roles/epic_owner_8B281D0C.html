Role: Epic Owner
Epic Owners are responsible for coordinating portfolio Epics through the Portfolio Kanban system. They define the epic, its Minimum Viable Product (MVP), and Lean business case, and when approved, facilitate implementation.
Role Sets: Roles
Relationships
Primary Performs
  • Defining The Epic Value Statement
  • Presenting the Epic
  • Shepherding Epics
  • Sizing Epic and Providing Input
Main Description

If an epic is accepted, the Epic Owner works directly with the Agile Release Train (ART) and Solution Train stakeholders to define the Features and Capabilities that realize the value. They may also have some responsibility for supporting the initiative as it moves downstream through the Continuous Delivery Pipeline to Release on Demand.




Staffing
SkillsMust be effective in collaborating closely with other groups in the enterprise
Assignment Approaches In SAFe, epics drive a significant amount of the economic value for the enterprise. Epic Owners are responsible for formulating and elaborating the epic, and analyzing its cost and impact. They define an MVP where applicable and secure approval (or rejection) of the epic.

The Epic Owner role in SAFe is just that—a responsibility assumed by an individual—not a job title.  The Epic Owner assumes the responsibilities outlined in the paragraphs below.
Key Considerations
Epic Owners can create a realistic and compelling vision, appropriate economic priorities, and consistent set of features and capabilities.
