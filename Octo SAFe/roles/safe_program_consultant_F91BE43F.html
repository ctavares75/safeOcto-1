Role: SAFe Program Consultant
SAFe Program Consultants (SPCs) are change agents who combine their technical konwledge of SAFe with an intrinsic motivation to improve the company's software and systems development processes. They play a critical role in successfully implementing SAFe. SPCs come from numerous internal or external roles, including business and technology leaders, portfolio/program/project managers, process leads, architects, analysts, and consultants.
Synonyms: Practitioner, manager, change agent
