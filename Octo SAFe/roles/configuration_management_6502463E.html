Role: Configuration Management
This role posseses the skillset for Shared Services.
Role Sets: Agile Release Train (ART), Shared Services
Relationships
Additionally Performs
  • Inspect and Adapt Workshops
  • Participating in Post-PI Planning
  • Participating in Pre-PI Planning
  • Program Increment (PI) Planning
Main Description
Shared Services represents the specialty roles, people, and services that are necessary for the success of an Agile Release Train (ART) or Solution Train but that cannot be dedicated full-time.
