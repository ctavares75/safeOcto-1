Task: Program Increment (PI) Planning
Program Increment (PI) Planning is a cadence-based, face-to-face event that serves as the heartbeat of the Agile Release Train (ART), aligning all the teams on the ART to a shared mission and Vision. For geographically distributed ARTs, the event may occur at multiple locations simultaneously by maintaining constant audio and video communication between the sites.
