Task: Assigning Business Value
Assigning business value during planning provides an essential face-to-face dialogue between the team and their most important stakeholders, the Business Owners. This is an opportunity to develop personal relationships between Agile teams and Business Owners, identify common concerns around which to gain mutual commitment, and better understand business objectives and their value.
Relationships
RolesPrimary Performer:
  • Business Owner
Additional Performers:
Main Description

Typically, this consists of ranking the user-facing features highest. But they also should include technical experts who know that architectural and other concerns will increase the team's velocity in producing future business value.
