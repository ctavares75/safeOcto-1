Task: Managing and Prioritizing the Flow of Work
Product Management manages the flow of work through the program Kanban and into the program backlog. Product Management is resonsible for making sure that there are enough features ready in the backlog at all times. For example, they have a feature acceptancecriteria that can be used to establish that it meets its Definition of Done (DoD). And since judicious selection and sequencing of features is the key economic driver for each ART, the backlog is reprioritized with WSJF before each Program Increment (PI) Planning session.
