Task: Building an Effective Product Management/Product Owner Team
Though the Product Owner and Product Management roles may report to different organizations, forming an effective extended Product Management/Product Owner team is the key to efficient and effective development. Such a team also contributes materially to the job satisfaction that comes with being a part of a high-performing team, one that routinely delivers on its quality and vision commitments.
