Concept: Built-In Quality
Built-In Quality practices ensure that each Solution element, at every increment, meets appropriate quality standards throughout development.

Main Description
Built-in quality is not unique to SAFe. Rather, it is a core principle of the Lean-Agile Mindset, where it helps avoid the cost of delays associated with the recall, rework, and defect fixing.The built-in quality philosiphy applies systems thinking to optimize the system as a whole, ensuring a fast flow across the entire Value Stream, and makes quality everyone's job.
